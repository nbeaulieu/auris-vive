# Auris Vive

> *"The music was always this alive."*

Auris Vive is a real-time music intelligence and visualisation platform. AI listens to any audio stream, separates it into its constituent instruments, transcribes every note, and renders living generative visuals — all simultaneously, all in sync.

Embedded in Apple Music, Spotify, or standalone. Built for musicians, consumers, and the space between focus and feeling.

---

## What it does

A song enters. Auris Vive hears it as four simultaneous layers — drums, bass, vocals, and everything else. Each layer becomes its own visual world. Tap a stem, and the entire visual field shifts to that instrument alone. Paul Chambers on bass fills the screen. Miles Davis on trumpet takes over.

The visuals aren't decorations. They're a translation — sound made visible in real time, driven by the actual notes being played.

For a musician it's anatomy. For a listener it's immersion. For someone who needs to focus, rest, or breathe — it's an environment.

## The name

**Auris** — Latin for *ear.* Sonic intelligence. The AI underneath.

**Vive** — French and Latin for *alive, living.* The visuals. The feeling. The imperative: *live.*

Together: *the living ear.* Or read as a command: *hear it alive.*
