AURIS Vive
Hear it. See it. Feel it.
Brand pillars
Intelligence
AI listens deeper than any human ear. Every stem, every note, every breath between beats — extracted and understood in real time.
Presence
Visuals that breathe with the music. Not a visualiser — a living translation. Each song has never looked the same twice.
Stillness
For focus, meditation, anxiety relief — Auris Vive finds the calm inside the sound. The music becomes an environment you can rest inside.

Tagline candidates
"Hear it. See it. Feel it."
The three pillars in sequence. Builds. Works as a campaign line and an onboarding moment.
"Music made visible."
Direct, functional. Strong for App Store copy and embedded contexts (Apple Music, Spotify).
"Every song has a shape."
Evocative. Invites curiosity. Good for launch campaign and social. More poetic than functional.
"The music was always this alive."
Revelation framing. Implies Auris Vive reveals something that was already there. Premium, confident.
